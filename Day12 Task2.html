Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh


Rebel Poet

kazi Nazrul is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as bidrohi kobi (robel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous Poet, musician and philospher of Bangladesh. The famous writer Nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of the period of the British government.His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised the voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and get popularity swiftly around the indian Subcontinent.


Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity,and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigorty and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his carrer, Nazrul wrote short stories,novels and essays but is best known his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records.) collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount"-Kazi Nazrul Islam
